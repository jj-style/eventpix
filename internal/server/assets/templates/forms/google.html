Google Drive Storage Configuration

  1. Open the desired folder in Google Drive
  2. Look at your address bar to find a URL like https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1aBcDeFgHiJkLmNoPQrStUvWxZy
  3. The folder ID is the string of characters after folders/. In the example above, this is 1aBcDeFgHiJkLmNoPQrStUvWxZy
